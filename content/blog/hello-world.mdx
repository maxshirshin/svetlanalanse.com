# Welcome to My New Website

I'm delighted to share my brand-new website with you. After years of showcasing my work on various platforms, I've built a dedicated home for my botanical art, writings, and teaching.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Gallery** — Browse my collections of botanical watercolours, oil paintings, and miniature works
- **Blog** — Thoughts on technique, inspiration, and the art of observing nature
- **Shop** — Limited-edition prints, original artworks, and workshop bookings
- **News** — Exhibition dates, workshop announcements, and awards

## About My Practice

My journey as a botanical artist began with studying the masters — Pierre Joseph Redouté's exquisite watercolours and the pioneering natural history illustrations of Maria Sibylla Merian. I spent years painting from real specimens in botanical gardens, learning to capture the subtle details that make each plant unique.

I look forward to sharing more of my work and process with you here. Feel free to [get in touch](/contact) if you have any questions.

*— Svetlana*
